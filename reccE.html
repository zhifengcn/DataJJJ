Why

        Resource and environmental carrying capacity is an important basis for balancing the coordinated development of human-land relations. It is a regional issue and a global issue. It has become one of the most import-ant indicators for judging regional sustain-able development.

 

 

How

        Relying on the big data of resource net-work to gain insight into regional reso-urce and environment issues, the region integrates data and model methods of network big data , social economy, ecolog-ical environment and resources to carry out comprehensive evaluation of resource and environment carrying capacity

 

 

Where

        The platform selected beijing-tianjin-hebei city group as the experimental area. The beijing-tianjin-hebei metropolitan area is one of the areas with the most dynamic economy, the highest degree of openness and the largest population absorption in China. Faced with the continuous progress of urbanization and environmental pollu-tion problems such as smog, carrying out the assessment of resource and environm-ent carrying capacity is of great significan-ce for regional collaborative development.

 

 

When

        this study assessed the resource and environmental carrying capacity of the beijing-tianjin-hebei regionBased on the data of environmental covariates in 2015.
